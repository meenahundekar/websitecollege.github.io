BE Information Science and Engineering Project

The BE Information Science and Engineering projects topics are based on workshops and seminars conducted by universities in collaboration with companies. Graduates are ready to work in a real-world environment. The projects allow them to demonstrate their abilities and strategies while also motivating them to deal with real-world situations. Some of the projects are listed below:
  • Android Controlled Notice Board Project
  • Android Based Home Automation System
  • Android Controlled Remote AC Power Control

Information Science and Engineering

The department of Information Science and Engineering was established in the year 1999 with an initial intake of 30 students. With the growth in demand from student fraternity the intake has been enhanced to strength of 120. The department is a pioneer in offering B.E. in Information Science and Engineering course in northern Karnataka. The department has well-qualified, experienced and enthusiastic faculty members. Many faculty members have published technical papers in different national and international conferences and also in refereed journals. The department has well equipped independent laboratories with the state-of-art computing facilities and with necessary software. RISE – Rays of information Science and Engineers – is a forum of ISE students to organize activities like Programming Contests, Technical/cultural fests, Paper Presentations, Quizzes, Seminars, Sports and Cultural competitions etc.
