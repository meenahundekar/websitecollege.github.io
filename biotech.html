Biotechnology

Department of Biotechnology, Basaveshwar Engineering College (A), Bagalkot, established in the year 2002 with a vision to make an impact through effective teaching learning process, basic research, technology based training and service to the society. The Department of Biotechnology offers UG, PG Diploma, PG and Ph.D programs. Department is recognized as R&D centre by VTU Belagavi since 2011. Department has well equipped independent laboratories, class rooms, seminar hall and library. Department is associated with industries which provides ample opportunities to students to work on internship, projects, explore opportunities in the industry.
