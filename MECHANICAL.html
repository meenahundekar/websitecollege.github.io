BE Mechanical Engineering Course Structure

Mechanical Engineering is one of the oldest and diverse fields of Engineering. A program in Mechanical Engineering encompasses elements from an array of fields and imparts extensive knowledge in subjects like Thermodynamics, Mechatronics, Solid Mechanics, Business Economics, Robotics and Biomedical Engineering. From Aerospace and Automobiles to Space Technology and Electrical Science, Mechanical Engineering touches almost every aspect of our lives. If you are thinking to pursue a program in this field, it is important to understand the structure of the course and here we have brought you all the key subjects comprising the Mechanical Engineering syllabus of a 4-year degree course

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering Department is one of the oldest and biggest departments of the institute. The department started the UG program (B.E. in Mechanical Engineering) with an initial intake of 30 students in 1963 with an affiliation to Karnataka University (KUD), Dharwad. The present intake is 120 and the department was granted autonomous status by Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi and University Grants Commission (UGC), New Delhi in the year 2007-2008.
