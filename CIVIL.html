BE Civil Engineering Course Structure

The course consists of four years divided into 8 semesters. In the primary year, students are added to primary essential topics. In addition, realistic lab classes improve the information of theoretical concepts. Project submission through the stop of the fourth semester is obligatory as in step with the curriculum. Both core and elective papers are available in this course.

Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering Department (CED) is one of the pioneering departments ever since the inception of the college in 1963. The department is unremittingly engaged in imparting quality education, producing outstanding graduates to meet the societal needs through its qualified faculty and state-of-art infrastructure. Department offers 4 years BE course with 120 intake and 2 years M.Tech courses in Environmental Engineering, Geo-Technical Engineering and Structural Engineering with 18 in take in each course. Department is recognized as a Research Centre by VTU since 2003 and as QIP Research Centre (Ph.D) by AICTE New Delhi in the year 2012. The department is recognized as a State Resource Centre to offer training in Earthquake Engineering under National Programme for Capacity Building of Engineers in Earthquake Risk Management (NPCBEERM).
